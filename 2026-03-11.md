# Cybersecurity News - 2026-03-11

## Major Breaches
* **FortiGate Devices Exploited:** Threat actors are targeting FortiGate NGFW appliances to extract configuration files containing service account credentials and network topology info, heavily impacting healthcare and government sectors.
* **Ericsson US Data Breach:** Ericsson Inc. disclosed a breach impacting over 15,000 employees and customers following a hack on one of its service providers.
* **UNC4899 Crypto Breach:** A North Korean threat actor breached a crypto firm using social engineering and an AirDropped trojanized file, eventually pivoting to the cloud to steal millions.

## AI in Security
* **Malicious OpenClaw Installer:** A malicious npm package posing as an AI agent (OpenClaw) installer was found deploying a remote access trojan (RAT) to steal macOS credentials, browser data, and crypto wallets.

## APTs
* **APT28 (Russia):** The state-sponsored group has been observed using customized implants (BEARDSHELL and COVENANT) to conduct long-term surveillance on the Ukrainian military.
* **Signal/WhatsApp Hijacking:** The Dutch government warned that Russian state-sponsored hackers are targeting government officials and journalists in an ongoing campaign to hijack Signal and WhatsApp accounts.

## Policy & Legal
* **CISA Directive on Ivanti:** CISA has flagged a high-severity Ivanti Endpoint Manager (EPM) vulnerability as actively exploited and ordered U.S. federal agencies to patch within three weeks.
* **Tycoon2FA Dismantled:** Europol announced the dismantling of the infrastructure behind the Tycoon2FA service and LeakBase operations, a significant win against adversary-in-the-middle phishing platforms.
